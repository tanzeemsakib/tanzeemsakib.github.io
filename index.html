About

I'm a graphic designer and visual artist based in Dhaka, Bangladesh, working across branding, illustration, motion, and UI. My work tends toward the editorial — restrained palettes, deliberate composition, ideas that stay with you.

Alongside design, I have a background in applied mathematics at the University of Dhaka — which informs how I approach problems: systematically, but with room for the unexpected.

Currently interning at BJIT Limited in the Branding-IR-PR team. Open to freelance commissions and full-time opportunities.
