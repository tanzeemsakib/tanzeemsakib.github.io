About

Designing
with intent

I'm a graphic designer and visual artist based in Dhaka, Bangladesh, working across branding, illustration, motion, and UI design. My work tends toward the editorial — restrained palettes, deliberate composition, ideas that stay with you.

Alongside design, I have a background in applied mathematics at the University of Dhaka — which informs how I approach problems: systematically, but with room for the unexpected.

Available for freelance commissions and open to full-time opportunities in graphic design, motion, or visual art.
